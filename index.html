What is the School Bell Platform?

The School Bell Platform allows administrators to schedule bells, manage holidays, and make live announcements for your school. You can automate your daily bell schedule, set up special events, and ensure that your school operates smoothly and on time.

How to Use It

1. Access the platform. First, test the bell so that the audio connection is initialized. Then you can start scheduling daily or weekly bells.
2. Set holidays so that bells do not ring on special days.
3. Use the Announcements section to play live microphone audio or custom chimes.
4. Monitor emergency alarms and send notifications as needed.
